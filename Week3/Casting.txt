Casiting     (upcasting and downcasting)
	- Converting a datatype to another (int to long) 
		- implicit:  If the original datatype has a smaller size than the target datatype (int to long)         (AutomaticCasting)
		- explicit:   if the original datatype has a larger size than the target datatype. 		     (ExplicitCasting)
			int i = (int) [long];
